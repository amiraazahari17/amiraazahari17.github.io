Artist of the Month

DSam Smith, born as Samuel Frederick Smith on 19 May 1992 is an English singer-songwriter. He rose to fame in October 2012 when he was featured on Disclosure's breakthrough single "Latch", which peaked at number eleven on the UK Singles Chart. He released In The Lonely Hour, his debut studio album in May 2016 on Capitol Records UK. It's single "Money on My Mind", became his second number one single in the UK after his feature on Naughty Boy's single "La La La".
